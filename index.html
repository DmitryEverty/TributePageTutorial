Avicii

The man who changed the world of electronic music forever

Tim Bergling, known professionally as Avicii, was a Swedish electronic musician, DJ, and songwriter, who specialized in audio programming, remixing and record producing.

Here are some facts about Tim:

Tim Bergling was born on 8 September 1989 in Stockholm, Sweden.

He began making music at the age of 16.

In May 2007, Bergling signed on with the Dejfitts Plays label.

By 2009 to 2010, Bergling was a prolific producer and was releasing music incredibly quickly.

Then, in 2010, Bergling released the hit song "Seek Bromance", which reached the top 20 in several countries.

In 2011, Bergling released "Levels", which launched him into the mainstream.

In 2012, his collaboration track "Sunshine" with David Guetta was nominated for a Grammy award under the category for Best Dance Recording.

Bergling was nominated for a Grammy for Best Dance Recording with "Levels" at the 2013 Grammy Awards.

Bergling died on 20 April 2018 near Muscat, Oman, at the age of 28.
